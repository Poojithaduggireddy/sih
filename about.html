Objective

When forest land needs to be diverted for developmental projects, having an accurate understanding of the tree population within the affected area is crucial. Traditional methods, such as manual surveys, are time-consuming, expensive, and prone to errors. Our solution leverages cutting-edge technology to address these challenges.
